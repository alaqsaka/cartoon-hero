About Rick and Morty

Rick and Morty is an American adult animated science fiction adventure comedy series created by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon for Cartoon Network's late-night programming block Adult Swim. In May 2018, the series was picked up for an additional 70 episodes over an unspecified number of seasons.
